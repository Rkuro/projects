Crypto

Crypto is a blockchain based decentralized application being developed on the EOS infrastructure. EOS is (theoretically) capable of 50,000 - 100,000 transactions per second, making it one of the highest scaling blockchain platforms in the market. This allows users to interact with the application with both low fees and high transaction throughput.
